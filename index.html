Animal monitoring and surveillance

This project was development for Goverenment Proposal.

  • Night Vision Surveillance: Integrated CCTV cameras with night vision capabilities to monitor forest areas bordering villages in the Sathyamangalam Forest near Erode.
  • Animal Detection and Alerts: Utilized advanced computer vision algorithms to detect animals entering villages and activated an alarm system to alert humans of potential threats.
  • Proactive Wildlife Management: Implemented sound systems designed to drive animals back into the forest, providing a proactive solution for wildlife management and ensuring human safety.
